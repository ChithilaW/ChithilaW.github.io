Our Story

Founded in 2015, Green Haven began as a small plant nursery with a passion for bringing nature into urban spaces. What started as a hobby quickly grew into a thriving business as we discovered the perfect pairing of plants with handcrafted ceramic pots.

Today, we collaborate with local artisans and sustainable growers to offer unique combinations that transform any space into a green oasis. Each piece in our collection is carefully selected for quality, beauty, and sustainability.
